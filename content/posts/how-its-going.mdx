## 👋 About Me

My interest in technology began when I was in elementary school. The first software I used was Friendster, and I was fascinated by how the chat system worked and the technology behind it. This passion grew stronger when I was in high school—playing Dota sparked my curiosity about how they built such a complex game. This early fascination led me to explore programming. I started learning Visual Basic 6.0, developed a foundation in PHP, and worked on various projects including a social media management system.

In 2016, I pursued my Computer Science degree at Eulogio "Amang" Rodriguez Institute of Science and Technology in Manila. I completed my thesis where I created a **drag-and-drop IDE for Java** that could connect to different database engines such as Access and others. This innovative application was developed in **C#** and designed to generate Java source code, streamlining the development process for database-driven applications.

Currently, I'm working as a Software Engineer at **NCS-PH**. I'm also actively working on a project to become a Quant Developer, combining my passion for trading and crypto with software engineering.
